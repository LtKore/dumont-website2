Meet our wonderful stylists!

Edwin

Edwin started out in the hotel business and worked at that for 15 years. Deciding it was time to further discover his passion for hair. He started out in the business at the bottom, and worked his way up to a now 25 long years at the House of Dumont. Edwin worked for Elizabeth Arden as an educator, and then moved to Wella as a colour technician, and business development specialist. All done with his love for the creative and business side of the industry. During Edwin's spare time, he enjoys writing children's books and cycle. He has cycled to Montreal three times and once to Niagara Falls for fundraisers. He loves to read and cook for my husband and friends.

Dianne

For Dianne, was started as playing with Barbie dolls grew into a passion for hairstyling. Dianne started her career in Toronto where she worked for Wella and Elizabeth Arden. Moving to San Francisco, California to further her career. She specializes in Blondes, Highlight, and Balayage. After 25 years, she is now back in Toronto and would love for you to be one of her clients. When not in the salon, Dianne enjoys bike riding, reading, cooking and strolling the beach.
